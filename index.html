Student Outcomes & Success Stories

Our diverse alumni network is making an impact across industries, driving innovation, leadership, and social change around the globe. Discover how our graduates are contributing to a brighter future.

Aerospace & Mechanical Engineering

Our graduates are leading space exploration at renowned institutions like NASA, ISRO, and SpaceX, working on cutting-edge projects like Mars rovers, space telescopes, and next-gen propulsion systems.

Civil & Environmental Engineering

Alumni are designing sustainable infrastructure, earthquake-resistant buildings, and eco-friendly urban development, improving lives in rapidly growing cities worldwide.

Electrical & Computer Engineering

From developing cutting-edge AI systems to revolutionizing renewable energy, our electrical engineers are shaping the future of technology and energy efficiency.

Computer Science & IT

Our computer science graduates are at the forefront of innovation, contributing to advancements in cloud computing, cybersecurity, and AI at tech giants like Google, Amazon, and Microsoft.

Healthcare & Biomedical Engineering

Innovating in medical technology, our graduates are developing life-saving treatments, cutting-edge diagnostic tools, and wearable health tech devices.

Design & Modern Media

Our creative minds are transforming industries with groundbreaking work in UX/UI design, digital media production, and interactive storytelling, redefining how we experience the world.

Business Management & Entrepreneurship

Our business graduates are leading global companies, launching successful startups, and driving corporate strategies that are shaping the world economy.

Law & Public Policy

Alumni in law and policy are advocating for social justice, influencing government policy, and serving in international organizations to uphold human rights and equality.

Industrial Leadership

Our graduates are transforming global industries with cutting-edge innovations in manufacturing, operations management, and sustainable production practices.
